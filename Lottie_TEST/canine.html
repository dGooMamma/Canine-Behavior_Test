M1: Introduction & Readings

This module is about separation anxiety in dogs.

Learning objectives:

1. To enhance your knowledge of the psychology of canine pathological behavior: this module will teach you about the psychological and behavioral principles related to canine separation anxiety.

2. To enhance your knowledge of behavioral modification: this module will introduce you to a canine model of human abnormal behavior (separation anxiety) and teach you about various ways this behavior can be modified through behavioral interventions.

3. To enhance your knowledge of clinical pharmacotherapy in the case of canine separation anxiety: this module will teach you about some of the medications used to treat separation anxiety in dogs. 

4. To enhance your critical and analytical thinking skills: this module will give you practice objectively evaluating case studies of pathological behavior in animals. 

 

Assignments

1. Read the article about separation anxiety in children and post a comment comparing it to separation anxiety in dogs

2. Ask 3 questions and reply to two peers who ask different questions about a case.

3. Take the case study quiz and find the errors.
